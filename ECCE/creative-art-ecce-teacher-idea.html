üß† Quick Tips for Art Activities

  • Managing mess: Use washable materials, cover tables with newspaper, and have wet wipes ready.
  • Color mixing: Instead of correcting, ask "What happens when we mix these colors?" to encourage exploration.
  • Process over product: Focus on the experience rather than the final result - it's about the creative journey.
  • Displaying work: Rotate artwork regularly so all children get a chance to see their creations displayed.
  • Reluctant artists: Offer alternative tools (like sponges instead of brushes) to help children engage.
