## How to Use

1. **Enter Website URL** - Provide your website URL (e.g., https://yourwebsite.com)
2. **Configure Traffic** - Set visitors per hour and duration
3. **Select Traffic Type** - Choose from Organic, Social, Direct, or Referral traffic
4. **Choose Target Countries** - Select specific countries or use "All Countries"
5. **Enable Features** - Toggle realistic bounce rates and AI-powered patterns
6. **Start Generation** - Click "Start Traffic Generation" to begin
7. **Monitor Progress** - Watch real-time statistics and analytics
