Group Management Guide

📌 How to Manage Two Groups Simultaneously

Step 1: Divide class into Group A (left side) and Group B (right side).

Step 2: Teach Group A for 15 min, give them independent work, then switch to Group B.

Step 3: Use peer tutoring - Group A helps Group B.

Step 4: Keep 5-minute buffer for transitions.

📅 Weekly Subject Rotation System

Monday: English + Math | Tuesday: Urdu + Science

Wednesday: Islamiat + Social Studies | Thursday: Art + Revision

Friday: Assessment + Group Discussion

⚠️ Difficulty Warning System

🔴 Hard subjects (Math, Science) should NOT be in last period

🟡 Medium subjects (English, Urdu) are flexible

🟢 Easy subjects (Art, Islamiat) are good for end of day

🎯 Quick Tips for Teachers

• Use visual timers for each activity

• Prepare independent work folders for each group

• Train group leaders to help manage

• Use local materials (stones, seeds, cardboard)
