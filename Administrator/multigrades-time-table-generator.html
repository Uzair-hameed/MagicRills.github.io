Teaching Strategies

ECCE (Early Childhood Care and Education)

Key Strategies: Play-based learning, storytelling, songs and rhymes, hands-on activities, sensory play.

Teacher Help: Use local materials for teaching aids, involve community elders for stories, pair older students as helpers.

Grades 1-2

Key Strategies: Phonics-based reading, number games, picture charts, group reading, flash cards.

Teacher Help: Peer teaching within class, use of local environment for examples, simple worksheets.

Grades 2-3

Key Strategies: Guided reading, math manipulatives, simple projects, role plays, picture composition.

Teacher Help: Group leaders can assist, use of educational posters, simple science experiments with local materials.

Grades 4-5

Key Strategies: Independent reading, problem-solving exercises, group discussions, written assignments, concept mapping.

Teacher Help: Student monitors, library corners, project-based learning, peer correction of work.

Multigrade Classroom Management

Key Strategies: Station teaching, learning centers, peer tutoring, flexible grouping, independent work folders.

Teacher Help: Train student leaders, create self-checking materials, use visual schedules, implement routine procedures.
